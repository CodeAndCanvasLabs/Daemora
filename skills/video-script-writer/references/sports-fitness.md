# Sports & Fitness Domain Reference

## 🔥 Trending Angles
- "I trained like [pro athlete] for 30 days — honest results"
- "The training mistake that's keeping you weak (and how to fix it)"
- "Why cardio isn't making you lose weight"
- "The sport science behind [athlete]'s dominance"
- "Breaking down the greatest [play/moment] in [sport] history"
- "Zone 2 training: why every elite athlete does it"
- "I tested every pre-workout. Here's the truth."
- "Beginner to intermediate in 90 days: the exact program"

## ⚡ Hook Formulas
- "You're not overtrained. You're under-recovered. Here's the difference."
- "This athlete runs faster at 35 than at 25. Here's exactly why."
- "I gained 12 pounds of muscle in 6 months. Here's every mistake I made."
- "The lift that fixes almost every athletic weakness — and nobody does it."

## 🎥 B-Roll
- Training footage (gym, field, track)
- Slow-motion movement breakdown (biomechanics)
- Heart rate / performance data overlay
- Meal prep / nutrition close-ups
- Athlete interviews (talking head)
- Game/competition highlights
- Before/after body composition (with consent framing)

## Compliance Notes
- Include "consult a physician before beginning any exercise program" for training videos
- Don't promise specific results: "results vary" framing
- Supplement content: note these are not FDA-evaluated claims

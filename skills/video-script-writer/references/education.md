# Education & E-Learning Domain Reference

## 🔥 Trending Angles
- "Learn [skill] in one video — the complete beginner guide"
- "The learning technique that 10x'd my retention"
- "Why most online courses fail their students"
- "I learned [hard skill] in 30 days — here's the exact process"
- "The Feynman Technique: learn anything faster"
- "What a $50,000 degree teaches vs what you actually need"
- "How to go from zero to job-ready in [field] in 6 months"
- "The spaced repetition system I wish I knew in school"

## ⚡ Hook Formulas
- "In the next 15 minutes, you'll understand [complex topic] better than most people who've studied it for years."
- "The way schools teach [subject] is completely backwards. Here's why."
- "I failed [exam/course] 3 times. Then I changed one thing."
- "This is the video I wish existed when I started learning [skill]."

## Structure: Tutorial / Course Module
1. Hook: Competency preview ("By the end, you'll be able to...")
2. Why this matters: Real-world application
3. The mental model: Big picture framework first
4. Core content (chunked into 3–5 clear steps)
5. Common mistakes: What trips people up
6. Practice exercise: Give them something to DO
7. What's next: Natural continuation / CTA

## Tone Notes
- Patience and clarity over efficiency — never rush through a concept
- Check in with the viewer: "Make sense so far?" "Pause here and try it"
- Celebrate small wins within the video — learning is emotional, not just intellectual

# Real Estate & Property Domain Reference

## 🔥 Trending Angles
- "I toured 50 apartments — here's what $2,000/month actually gets you in [city]"
- "The hidden costs of buying a home nobody warns you about"
- "Why smart investors are buying in [market] right now"
- "House flipping in 2025 — the honest truth"
- "I analyzed 1,000 rental listings. Here's what I found."
- "The negotiation tactics agents use (that buyers can use too)"
- "Airbnb vs long-term rental — which makes more money?"
- "How to spot a money pit property before you buy"

## ⚡ Hook Formulas
- "This house looks like a $300k home. It sold for $1.1 million. Here's why."
- "I almost made a $40,000 mistake buying my first home. Here's what stopped me."
- "Landlords don't want you to know this about lease negotiations."
- "The neighborhood that's about to explode in value — and how to find the next one."

## 🎥 B-Roll
- Property exterior walk (drone + ground)
- Interior room-by-room walkthrough
- Before/after renovation splits
- Neighborhood aerial footage
- Price history graphs (animated)
- Signing documents (close-up)
- Keys exchanging hands

## Compliance Note
- Always say "past performance does not guarantee future returns" for investment content
- Never promise specific returns or appreciation rates

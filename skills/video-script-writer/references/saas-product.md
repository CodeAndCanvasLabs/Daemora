# SaaS & Product Demo Domain Reference

## 🔥 Trending Angles
- "We went from 0 to $10k MRR in 90 days — here's exactly how"
- "Why we rebuilt our onboarding from scratch (and what happened)"
- "Demo: [Product] in 5 minutes — everything you need to know"
- "The feature our users didn't ask for — that they can't stop using"
- "How [company] reduced churn by 40% with one UX change"
- "I tested 8 [tool category] tools — here's the honest ranking"
- "Building in public: month 3 update"

## ⚡ Hook Formulas
- "This tool just made [job title] irrelevant. Or did it?"
- "We almost shut down. Here's what saved us."
- "Your team is wasting 2 hours a day on something [product] fixes in 30 seconds."
- "The fastest demo of [product] you'll ever watch."

## 🎥 B-Roll / Visuals
- Clean screen recordings (no clutter, cursor movements highlighted)
- Dashboard with live data (anonymize sensitive info)
- Before/after workflow comparison
- Team using product in Slack / Notion / real environment
- Animated product walkthrough (Loom-style)
- Testimonial-style talking head (customer voice)

## Structure: Product Demo Video
1. Hook: The problem it solves (show pain, not product)
2. "What if..." — paint the better world
3. Introduce the product: Name + one-line value prop
4. Demo: Feature 1 (most impressive)
5. Demo: Feature 2 (most useful daily)
6. Demo: Feature 3 (differentiator from competitors)
7. Pricing / how to start
8. CTA: Free trial / book demo

## Tone Notes
- Lead with the customer's problem, never the product's features
- Concrete numbers always: "saves 3 hours/week" not "saves time"
- Trust signals matter: mention customers, integrations, security if relevant

# Spirituality & Wellness Domain Reference

## 🔥 Trending Angles
- "I meditated every morning for 90 days — here's what actually changed"
- "The science behind why [spiritual practice] works"
- "Shadow work explained: what it is and how to start"
- "Nervous system regulation: the skill nobody teaches you"
- "Why journaling alone isn't enough (and what to add)"
- "The difference between spirituality and religion — a calm conversation"
- "Manifestation vs action: where the line actually is"
- "Somatic healing: what your body is trying to tell you"

## ⚡ Hook Formulas
- "Your anxiety isn't a problem to fix. It's a message. Here's how to listen."
- "I haven't felt overwhelmed in 6 months. Here's the only thing that changed."
- "Most meditation advice is wrong. Here's what actually works for anxious minds."
- "You're not lazy. Your nervous system is dysregulated. Here's the difference."

## 🎥 B-Roll
- Person meditating (soft natural light, outdoors or clean interior)
- Journaling close-up (handwriting, morning light)
- Nature footage (forest, ocean, clouds moving)
- Candles, incense, crystals (aesthetic stillness)
- Breathing visualization (abstract animation)
- Slow-motion water, flowers, movement

## Tone Notes
- Never prescriptive about beliefs — "some find that..." "many people experience..."
- Trauma-informed language: avoid "just do this" — offer invitations, not commands
- Calm pacing even in long-form — this audience doesn't want to be rushed
- Disclaimer for mental health content: "This is not a substitute for professional mental health support"
- ASMR style works exceptionally well in this domain

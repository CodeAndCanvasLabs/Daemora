# Fashion & Beauty Domain Reference

## 🔥 Trending Angles
- "I wore [aesthetic] for 30 days — here's what happened"
- "Dupe vs designer: is it actually worth the price difference?"
- "The capsule wardrobe that works for everything"
- "How [brand] built a $1B brand through aesthetics alone"
- "The skincare ingredients that actually work (according to dermatologists)"
- "Getting ready with me: [event/occasion]"
- "I let AI style me for a week — honest review"
- "Sustainable fashion: what's real vs greenwashing"

## ⚡ Hook Formulas
- "I spent $50 and looked like I spent $500. Here's exactly how."
- "This skincare routine cleared my skin in 3 weeks. Dermatologist approved."
- "Everyone's wearing [trend] wrong. Here's the right way."
- "The one clothing item that makes every outfit look expensive."

## 🎥 B-Roll
- Outfit flat lay (overhead shot)
- Get-ready-with-me mirror footage
- Texture close-ups (fabric, skin, product)
- Product unboxing (clean, aesthetic setup)
- Street style / lifestyle montage
- Before/after split (makeup, styling)
- Shopping haul spread on bed

## Tone Notes
- Inclusive language — "all body types", "any skin tone"
- Be honest about product results — trust is the currency
- Visual quality matters more here than in any other domain — note it in production notes

## Compliance — ALWAYS Include
- "This video is for educational purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider."
- Never diagnose — use "may indicate", "could suggest", "some people experience"
- Cite study types: "a 2024 randomized controlled trial found..."
- Mental health content: include crisis line reference if topic is sensitive

- Person in calm doctor consultation (not alarmist)
- Lab / research setting
- Person sleeping, exercising, eating (lifestyle)
- Infographic overlays (statistics, study results)
- Anatomical diagrams (clean, modern style)

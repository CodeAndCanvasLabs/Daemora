- "You're losing $X every year by doing nothing. Here's the math."
- "The investment strategy that beats 90% of fund managers — and anyone can do it."

## Compliance
- "This is not financial advice. Consult a licensed financial advisor."
- Never promise returns or guarantee outcomes
- Use "historically", "in general", "many investors find" not absolute claims

# Gaming & Esports Domain Reference

## 🔥 Trending Angles
- "I played [game] for 100 hours so you don't have to"
- "The build that broke the meta"
- "How [pro player] actually thinks — frame-by-frame breakdown"
- "This indie game is better than [AAA title] and nobody's talking about it"
- "The psychology behind why [game] is so addictive"
- "Every mistake new players make in [game] (and how to fix them)"
- "The history of [franchise] — how it went from $X to $Y"
- "I tried every [class/character] so here's the definitive tier list"
- "The hidden mechanics [game] never explains"

## ⚡ Hook Formulas
- "This clip looks impossible. It's not. Here's exactly how it's done."
- "I've been playing [game] wrong for 200 hours. Here's what changed."
- "[Game] just dropped a patch that broke everything. Let me explain."
- "The pro scene discovered something in [game] that changes everything."
- "This is the highest damage build in the game. And it's stupidly easy."

## 🎥 B-Roll / Gameplay Footage
- In-game footage (capture card or screen record)
- Slow-motion replays of key moments
- Split-screen: bad play vs good play
- HUD highlights with annotation overlays
- Pro match VOD clips (fair use commentary)
- Reaction cam (face cam in corner)
- Character/skin showcase
- Map overview with callouts labeled

## 📐 Structure Templates

### Game Review
1. Hook: Most impressive or controversial moment from the game
2. First impression: What it feels like in the first hour
3. Core gameplay loop: What you actually DO
4. What it does brilliantly: Specific, with examples
5. What frustrates: Honest critique
6. Who should buy it: Be specific (genre fans, fans of X)
7. Score / verdict + CTA

### Tips & Guide
1. Hook: The end result (highest level play / insane clip)
2. The core mistake most players make
3. Tip 1 (foundational)
4. Tip 2 (intermediate)
5. Tip 3 (advanced / surprising)
6. Putting it all together
7. Challenge the viewer: "Try this in your next match"

### Esports Analysis
1. Hook: The defining moment from the match/event
2. Context: Who's playing, what's at stake
3. The strategy breakdown: Phase by phase
4. The turning point: What changed everything
5. What teams/players can learn
6. Prediction/hot take for what's next
7. CTA

## 🎨 Tone Notes
- Speak the game's language — use in-game terms naturally
- Humor and personality are expected — gaming audience values authenticity
- Be direct with opinions — "This is bad" > "Some people might not enjoy this"
- Respect the audience — many know more than you, acknowledge that
- Energy level: match the game's energy (cozy game = calm voice, FPS = faster cuts)

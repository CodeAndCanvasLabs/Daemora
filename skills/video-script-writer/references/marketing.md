## ⚡ Hook Formulas
- "Your customers aren't buying your product. They're buying an identity."
- "I reverse-engineered 100 viral videos. Here's the formula."
- "This $200/day ad is outperforming our $10,000/day campaign. Here's why."
- "The copywriting principle that made Claude Hopkins a legend — still works today."

## Structure: Case Study Format
1. Hook: The result first ("We 6x'd revenue without increasing ad spend")
2. The starting problem: What was broken, with data
3. The hypothesis: What we decided to test and why
4. The execution: Exactly what we changed
5. The results: Raw data — be honest about what didn't work too
6. The lesson: One transferable principle
7. CTA
